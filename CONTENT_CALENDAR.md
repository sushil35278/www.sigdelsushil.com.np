## Strategy Notes

### Why These Topics Drive Revenue

- **Enterprise Intent**: All topics solve real business problems
- **B2B Keywords**: Advertisers paying $15-20 CPM for these keywords
- **Long-form Content**: 2000-3000 words increase session time, more ad impressions
- **Problem-Solving**: Tutorials with code drive high engagement

### Publishing Schedule

- **Consistency**: 2 posts per month minimum (14 total over 7 months)
- **Day**: Tuesday or Wednesday (best for tech content distribution)
- **Time**: 10 AM UTC (good for global audience)

### SEO Optimization

Each blog post should include:
- [ ] Internal links to 3-5 existing blog posts
- [ ] "Related Articles" section at bottom
- [ ] Table of Contents (H2 and H3 headings)
- [ ] Code snippets (minimum 2-3 examples)
- [ ] Conclusion with CTA linking to other posts

### Promotion Strategy

After each post publish:
1. Submit to Hacker News (if relevant)
2. Share on dev.to with backlink
3. Post on Reddit (r/programming, r/devops, r/kubernetes, r/AI)
4. Share on LinkedIn (tag companies/products mentioned)
5. Mention in relevant technical forums

### Monetization Metrics to Track

Track in Google Analytics 4:
- **Avg. Session Duration** (more time = more ad impressions)
- **Pages per Session** (internal links effectiveness)
- **Bounce Rate** (content quality indicator)
- **AdSense RPM** (revenue per 1000 impressions)
- **CTR** (click-through rate on ads)

### Ad Placement Strategy

- **Above fold**: 728x90 leaderboard or 300x250 medium rectangle
- **In-content**: 300x250 after first H2 heading
- **Below content**: Related posts with 300x250 on side
- **Responsive**: Use responsive ad units for mobile

### Expected Revenue (once approved)

Based on 1.52k visitors/month and optimized content:
- **CPM**: $10-15 average across all posts
- **CTR**: 2-3% average
- **Ad impressions per visitor**: 3-5
- **Estimated monthly**: $50-150 (growing with traffic)
